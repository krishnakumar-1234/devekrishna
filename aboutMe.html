Background:

A Humble Beginning

Growing up in a village, life took a challenging turn when my father passed away. Amidst the grief, my uncle emerged as more than a relative; he became my guiding light, my godsend. A Guardian Angel in Human Form Just ten days after my father's demise, my uncle took me under his wing and brought me to the city. Learning to Code:

A Self-Study Journey

In this new city life, my uncle became more than a caretaker; he became my coding mentor. Despite not having a formal background in programming, he encouraged and guided me through the world of self-study. Together, we embraced the challenges and triumphs of learning to code independently.


Educational Triumphs:

Self-Study and Support

My coding journey wasn't confined to traditional classrooms. My uncle's innovative approach to self-study empowered me to explore coding languages, tackle problems independently, and foster a deep love for the art of programming. It was a joint effort of determination and unwavering support.
